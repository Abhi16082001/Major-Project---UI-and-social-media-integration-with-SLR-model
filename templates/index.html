    Instructions:

  • Click on the button "Start Recognizing" below.
  • It will open the OpenCV window and you will be able to show the hand symbol to the camera.
  • Then Show any hand symbol to the camera and it will show on the screen.
  • It will be included in the sentence only if its accuracy will be greater than 99.00 percent.
  • To show next symbol move out the hand from the frame and then move in again.
  • To close the window, make it active and then press 'esc' key.
  • Then you will be redirected to Results Page and you will see your text in a Text Box.
  • You can also send this text to WhatsApp using "Send via WhatsApp" button.
